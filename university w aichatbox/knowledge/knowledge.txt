## Biography
Dr. Ali Al-Sinayyid is an Assistant Professor in Computer Science and the Director of the Cybersecurity Innovation Center (CIC) at WVSU. His expertise spans cybersecurity, IT management, and computer science, focusing on critical infrastructure protection, cybersecurity education, and innovation.

He has designed and led various cybersecurity programs, including a newly proposed Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity at WVSU. His research involves Industrial Control System (ICS) security, phishing attack defense, and the application of AI in cybersecurity.

Under his leadership, the WVSU CIC has become a hub for workforce development, training students through labs like **CyberHive**, **HoneyNet**, and **KITE**, which simulate real-world ICS environments.  
He also contributes to programs like the **Agriculture Cybersecurity and AI Lab**, addressing cybersecurity challenges in agriculture and related industries.

Dr. Ali has secured over **$4 million in grants** and built partnerships with companies such as **Google**, **IBM**, and **Amazon**. He has published numerous peer-reviewed papers and delivers talks at national conferences.
